Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help inform both the rising generations and those that are curios about the origin and history of Nintendos varies franchises. Many people are unfamilier with how old some of Nintendos games are and with the popularity of games like Super Smash Bros using many characters from these franchises people often want to know more.

Audience

Younger people that know little to nothing about the history of Nintendo and its varies IPs.

Content

Home page

When people first visit the website the first page that they will be sent to they will be greeted with a short description of what the site is and how it works. Just below that will be a vertical timeline for the release dates of various Nintendo Ips. Users will be able to click on an image of the title screen of the games that will send them to a page with more information about that particular game series. The reason for using a timeline is to make it easy to understand exactly when these franchises came out and the order that they were released in. As some of these franchises come out in close proximity to each other there won’t be a lot of room on the timeline for information so that is where other “series” page comes form. The most important information to make this site work is the original release dates, how many games there are and what was the latest release, as some of these franchises have not had much momentum over the last few decades. The biggest perk of this website over others is the timeline. Usually if you want to see if a game came out before another you would have to look at multiple webpages to see well with this website you get it at a glance. The main visitors will be people who have played some of Nintendo’s games on the switch and want to know just how old the series is or what other entries in the series there are.

Series

The other main page will be the “Series” page that contains information such as the release date in different regions of the world, how many main line games there are, brief overview about the story and gameplay of each game and other facts. Along with the descriptions there will also be a gallery with an image of the logo and box art or title screens of some of the titles in their series. There will also be a mini timeline showing the release date of all the mainline games in each series just below the discerption section. The reason for omitting spin off games is due to some series having a large number of spin off and side game with there being little space on the mini timeline for that large of number. Due to the large volume of Nintendo’s Ips not all of them will be represented, there will mostly be a focus on Nintendo’s major franchises and some of the more popular lesser franchises.

The general idea for this site is to help both the rising generation to know about the history and heritage of these Nintendo franchises as well as older people who don’t have a deep understanding of Nintendo Ips.
